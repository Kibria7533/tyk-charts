Thank you for installing Tyk OSS Chart {{ .Chart.Name }}. Your release is named {{ .Release.Name }}. At this point, Tyk Gateway is fully installed and should be accessible.

To access your gateway, you can port-forward gateway pod to 8080 on your machine:
    kubectl port-forward service/gateway-svc-{{ include "tyk-gateway.fullname" . }} 8080:{{ .Values.gateway.service.port }}
    
Run `curl localhost:8080/hello` to see some data back.
